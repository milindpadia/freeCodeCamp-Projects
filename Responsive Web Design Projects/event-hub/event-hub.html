Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Vinayak Chaturthi or Vinayak Chavithi or Vinayagar Chaturthi, is a Hindu festival celebrating the birthday of Hindu deity Ganesh. The festival is marked with the installation of Ganesh's murtis privately in homes and publicly on elaborate pandals.

Date: 27 August, 2025

Navaratri

Navaratri is an annual Hindu festival observed in honor of the goddess Durga, an aspect of Adi Parashakti, the supreme goddess. It spans over nine nights, first in the month of Chaitra, and again in the month of Ashvin.

Dates: 22 September 2025 - 02 October 2025

Hanuman Jayanti

Hanuman Jayanti, also called Hanuman Janmotsav, is a Hindu festival celebrating the birth of the Hindu deity, and one of the protagonists of the Ramayana and its many versions, Hanuman. The celebration of Hanuman Jayanti varies by time and tradition in each state of India.

Date: 12 April 2025

Maha Shivratri

Maha Shivaratri is a Hindu festival celebrated annually to worship the deity Shiv, between February and March. According to the Hindu calendar, the festival is observed on the fourteenth day of the first half of the lunar month of Phalgun.

Date: 26 February 2025
